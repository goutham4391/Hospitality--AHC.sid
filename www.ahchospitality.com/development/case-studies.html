          Case Studies

          At AHC Hospitality, we treat every property as if it were our own. View our case studies for an overview of a few short-term projects we've managed in addition to our long-term portfolio of Hotels.

          HAGGAI INSTITUTE - MAUI, HAWAII

          Focus: Renovation

          AHC Hospitality successfully orchestrated a major renovation of this global Christian training facility in Kihei, Maui, involving all 200 guest rooms, public areas, meeting facilities, swimming pool, outdoor landscaping, mechanical, and electrical infrastructure. This involved coordinating communication efforts with corporate leadership, operations team, architect, designers, vendors, and construction team. We also assisted the operations team in developing a preventative maintenance plan for all engineering and operations equipment.

          In addition to facilitating capital project funding between major donors and the corporate office, we developed a short-term and long-term capital plan for the facility. AHC Hospitality also worked closely with the operations team, local vendors, and construction trades in order to fulfill all objectives. This also included the execution of all purchasing of equipment and materials including shipping, delivery, and installation logistics.

          Haworth hotel

          Focus: Rebrand and Market Analysis

          Throughout a complete renovation of the once Haworth Inn and Conference Center, AHC Hospitality partnered with ownership to develop a thorough Sales and Marketing Plan as well as a new, comprehensive brand identity. Based on market analysis, we identified key strategies to meet ownership KPIs in the short- and long term. We established a new revenue management approach, sales structure, and key marketing tactics for the re-launch of the newly branded, Haworth Hotel.

          In addition, AHC Hospitality also delivered a complete brand identity that included the property logo, brand voice and tone, copy, color palette, typography guide, photo direction, advertising, and creative direction.
