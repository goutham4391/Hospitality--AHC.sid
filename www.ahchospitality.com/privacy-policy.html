          COOKIE POLICY & DECLINING COOKIES

          As is commonly done on websites, this Site may use cookies and similar technology to customize your Site experience and keep track of those using the Site. A "cookie" is a small text file containing alphanumeric characters that is stored on your computer and uniquely identifies your browser. You consent to our cookies if you continue to use our Site. If you do not wish to allow cookies, please disable cookies by changing your browser settings before continuing to use this website. Please be aware that some of the Site’s functions or features may not work properly without cookies. The cookies we use are:

          • _ga - Google Analytics. used to distinguish unique users by assigning a randomly generated number as a client identifier. It is included in each page request in a site and used to calculate visitor, session and campaign data for the sites analytics reports.
          • _gat_UA - Google Analytics. Used to throttle request rate.
          • _gid - Google Analytics. Stores and update a unique value for each page visited to distinguish users.

          If you sign up to receive our informational e-mails, we will also use cookies in conjunction with these emails. When your browser downloads any graphic content in the e-mail, we will also place a cookie on your computer that will tell us if you come to our website at a later date.

          In addition to our own cookies, we collaborate with various third-party service providers who also use cookies to help us optimize our website and understand more about the visitors to our website. For example, we use Google Analytics to provide us with demographic information about our visitors and to help us analyze how people use our website. Our third-party service providers include Adobe Analytics, Google Analytics, and Facebook Pixel, and Campaign Monitor. To learn more about how these providers use cookies, please click the links above or visit their respective websites to view their privacy policies.

          CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

          For California residents: We may share your personal information with affiliated third parties, some of which do not share the AHC name, for their direct marketing purposes. These categories of affiliate are considered unaffiliated parties under California law, you may request information about our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please contact us as detailed below. Please include your name and email address in such request.

          We do not sell your information. However, as a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") you still have certain rights. These rights include:

          • Right of disclosure: We must disclose the personal information collected, sold, or disclosed for a business purpose about you, and cannot collect person information if you have not been informed that it will be collected and consent to its use. This Privacy Notice is intended to serve as our disclosure to you.
          • Right to access: You may request copies of specific personal information that we have collected, sold, or disclosed about you, and we must provide you with the personal information you request.
          • Right to be forgotten: You may request that we delete certain personal information we have collected about you, and we must delete that information once we verify your request, with certain exceptions.
          • Right to opt out: You may opt out of the sale of their personal information to third parties, and we must honor that request. Because we do not sell your information, you do not need to separately opt out of any sale by us.
          • Right to equal services: We may not discriminate against you if you choose to exercise the rights outlined above. This means we cannot deny you goods or services, charge you different prices or rates for goods or services, providing you with a different level or quality of goods or services, or suggesting you will receive a different price or rate for the goods or services or a different level or quality of goods or services.

          If you wish to exercise any of your rights under the CCPA, you may contact us using the contact details below. Please include in your email a sufficiently detailed description of the CCPA right with which you seek assistance. We may need to contact you for further information, and we will notify you if your request has been granted or declined, or if exceptions apply.
