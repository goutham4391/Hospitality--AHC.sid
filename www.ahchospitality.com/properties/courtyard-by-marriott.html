          Courtyard by Marriott Grand Rapids Downtown

          Recently remodeled and remarkably close to downtown Grand Rapids’ vibrant nightlife, the Courtyard by Marriott Grand Rapids Downtown offers easy access to downtown’s shopping, dining, and entertainment.

          Ideal for any guest, the skywalk offers convenient access to DeVos Place Convention Center, Van Andel Arena, as well as dozens of restaurants and nightclubs, making it the perfect home base for the convention crowd, business travelers, and pleasure seekers alike.

          Comfort and Convenience

          Fresh and inviting, our renovated rooms feature plush bedding with posh linens, a mini fridge, easy access outlets and comfortable lounging area. There’s also a roomy desk and plug-and-play connectivity via our "Jack Pack," which allows you to connect to our 37” HDTVs.

          Close to It All

          Located in the heart of the Entertainment District, Courtyard by Marriott Grand Rapids Downtwon is close to two of Grand Rapids’ most well-known bars, the B.O.B. and 20 Monroe Live. The hotel is also steps away from one of southwest Michigan’s largest concert and sporting event venues, the VanAndel Arena.

          Meetings, Weddings, and More

          The Courtyard by Marriott Grand Rapids Downtown features ample function and meeting spaces ideal for wedding receptions and ceremonies, as well as private events. Our on-site professional meeting planners are able to assist in planning everything from wedding receptions to providing discounted overnight accommodations for your guests.
