          Ruth's Chris Steak House Ann Arbor

          Situated in the active and charming heart of downtown, Ruth’s Chris Steak House of Ann Arbor, Michigan, offers an inviting setting for business meetings, romance, celebrations or just an evening out. Decorated with art produced by local artists, our Ann Arbor steak house takes full advantage of its locale through the use of many fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

          Welcome To Our Table

          At Ruth’s Chris Grand Rapids Ann Arbor we’re picky about our steaks and you should be picky about your steak houses. That’s why we serve only the ﬁnest USDA Prime beef available. Our steaks are hand-selected from the top 2% of the country’s beef, broiled to perfection and served the Ruth’s Chris signature way: sizzling on a 500 degree plate so that every last bite stays hot and delicious.

          Experience Ruth's Chris Hospitality

          Groups of nearly any size can experience extraordinary and intimate dining in specialized private dining spaces—some of our rooms showcase hometown pride with names honoring the nearby University of Michigan.
