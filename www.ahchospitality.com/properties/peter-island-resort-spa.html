          Peter Island Resort & Spa

          Named one of Forbes “10 Most Romantic Beach Resorts,” Peter Island Resort & Spa is a true island paradise. Boasting miles of sun-soaked sands, a world-class spa, and truly breathtaking accommodations, it’s the perfect setting for perfectly contented guests. Overstate its magnificence? Impossible.

          This is That Place. Paradise in Paradise.

          For those seeking a true British Virgin Islands vacation, no other destination in the world compares. Situated on 1,800 acres, Peter Island Resort & Spa is the epitome of barefoot elegance. A true haven of tranquility and retreat from the everyday.

          Caribbean Quarters

          Nothing quite captures the British Virgin Islands experience like sitting on a private, furnished balcony, soaking in the sweeping, panoramic views of the Caribbean. Offering a host of amenities, all rooms are climate controlled and feature ceiling fans, king size beds, writing desk, and comfortable reading chair. Personal amenities include plush waffle robes, Natura Bisse® bathroom products, a meticulously stocked minibar, iPod® alarm clock, and ice service twice daily.

          The newly renovated bathrooms feature an oversized vanity and large walk-in shower with pebble floors.

          Stunning Villas, Sublime Views

          Perched high atop the island’s most picturesque bluffs, our three luxurious villas offer panoramic views of unspoiled Caribbean beauty. Beyond the visible beauty is the welcoming hospitality our team goes out of our way to ensure. So from the moment guests walk through our doors, they’re greeted with truly personalized touches just for them.

          Additionally, a stay in the Crow’s Nest or Falcon’s Nest comes with a personal staff that includes a butler, a chef, and a housekeeper, as well as a chauffeur-driven vehicle

          HAWK'S NEST VILLA

          Overlooking the legendary Deadman’s Bay and Sprat Bay sits the Hawk’s Nest Villa. There, you can lounge beside a private pool that is just steps away from the resort’s sparkling pool and lobby. Or stroll down to the water’s edge and experience the warmth of our Caribbean waters. The Hawk’s Nest’s layout and location is ideal for families or those who desire convenience along with the luxury of a secluded private villa.

          CROW'S NEST VILLA

          Embraced by warm tropical breezes, the Crow’s Nest is set atop a cliff overlooking the turquoise waters of Sir Francis Drake Channel. With a grand yet comfortable open living area designed for entertaining, along with bedrooms boasting their own private entrances, the Crow’s Nest enables guests to enjoy time together or by themselves, all while surrounded by unspoiled Caribbean beauty.

          FALCON'S NEST VILLA

          The crown jewel of our private luxury villas overlooks White Bay Beach and offers endless views of Norman Island and distant St. John. A Barcelona-style villa estate, the Falcon’s Nest totals 21,645 interior square feet, comprised of three separate residences with six spacious suites, seven baths, and three half bathsNest totals 21,645 interior square feet.

          Water, Water Everywhere

          Five pristine white sand Caribbean beaches, each one with its own unique personality, ensure that each day at the beach is a totally different day at the beach.

          Be Deliciously, Delightfully Pampered.

          Rich with an abundance of indulgent services, the 10,000-square-foot Spa at Peter Island offers all the necessities to delight mind, body, and spirit. Among its many features are ten indoor treatment rooms, two outdoor bohios, and couple’s suites—all with spectacular views of Big Reef Bay. And as if massages, scrubs, wraps, facials, hair treatments, manicures, and pedicures weren’t enough, the spa specializes in Ayurvedic treatments, a traditional system of Indian medicine renowned for its detoxifying and rejuvenating properties.

          Beyond the treatments themselves, the spa is also an all-day getaway, offering lunch on the patio, gardens, private pool, hot tub, and yoga classes.
