          The Waterfront Inn

          A charming favorite among boutique hotels in The Villages®, Florida, The Waterfront Inn is situated on beautiful Lake Sumter, just two blocks from the very popular Town Square. Enjoy an evening exploring The Villages® or relax and unwind by our large outdoor swimming pool. Whether you’re here for a weekend getaway or weeklong stay, our attentive staff provides the finest in lakeside luxury.

          Charming Accommodations

          The Waterfront Inn is the perfect union of sophistication and relaxation. All guest rooms feature flat-screen televisions with cable, cozy pillow-top beds, spacious work desks, high-speed wireless internet, flat screen HD televisions, Keurig coffee makers, and plenty of plug in options for all devices.

          Views From the Waterfront

          The relaxing setting with waterfront views at Lake Sumter Landing make The Waterfront Inn a tranquil place to relax and the perfect setting for both business and pleasure.

          Impressive Events

          Our versatile meeting space is perfect for meetings, association clubs, corporate retreats, luncheons, weddings, and special occasions for up to 100 guests. Our professional Event Planning staff is ready to assist with every detail, including on-site catering.
