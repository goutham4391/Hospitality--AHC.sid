          Brownwood Hotel & Spa

          Located in The Villages® Florida, The Brownwood Hotel & Spa is a destination in itself. Built with intention and authenticity, it embraces the history of the Floridian cowboy with impeccable details everywhere you look, inviting guests to roam free while enjoying its distinctive accommodations and public spaces.

          Details for Days

          "It's all in the details" has never been truer than at The Brownwood Hotel and Spa. Wherever the eye falls, decor, comfort, and style have been meticulously addressed in a manner that truly delights. No matter what your style, you'll feel incredibly impressed by this hotel.

          Ahh-commdations

          Incorporating the spirit of the Floridian Cowboy, the hotel's guest rooms and suites are casually luxurious. Continuing to address every detail, the rooms are thoughtfully appointed, feature marble bath vanities, plush beds and furnishings in inviting patterns and textures, and all the little extras you look for in a home away from home.

          Extraordinary Events

          With 10,000 square feel of flexible meeting space, The Brownwood Hotel and Spa is the perfect setting for small and not-so-small gatherings alike. Board meetings, banquets, weddings, baby showers and conferences are all equally impressive, managed by an amazing team of meeting and banquet associates who aim to make every event your best one ever.
