          Amway Grand Plaza, Curio Collection by Hilton

          As one of AHC Hospitality’s most historic, charming properties, the Amway Grand Plaza, Curio Collection by Hilton is an incomparable destination. Beyond its famed architectural details, the legendary hotel is also celebrated for its hospitality and impeccable attention to detail.

          The Not-so-small Details

          Luxurious accommodations, keyless entry, spectacular views, intuitive amenities, service-oriented staff, a delicious range of food and beverage options, both in-room or on premises. Whatever you require is probably already in your room, but if there's something else needed, we’ll make sure you get it.

          Breathtaking, Inside and Out

          Wherever you look at the Amway Grand Plaza, Curio Collection by Hilton, your eyes will be delighted with sensational scenery both inside and out. Epic in every way, the historic Pantlind lobby boasts a stone fountain, stunning chandeliers, and the largest gold leaf ceiling in North America. Our ballrooms are also rich in detail and charm, varying from modern to classic to pure opulence. Accommodations also delight, with tower rooms providing sweeping views of the Grand River, stunning downtown Grand Rapids and breathtaking sunsets.

          For Any and Every Occasion

          With over 47,000 square feet of flexible meeting space, the Amway Grand Plaza, Curio Collection by Hilton can accommodate gatherings of five to five hundred or more. Conferences, banquets, weddings, showers or business meetings are all led by a team of experienced and dedicated meeting professionals and associates whose collective goal is assuring every meeting is as flawless as possible. Connected via skywalk to DeVos Place, a 234,000 square foot convention center, for which the Amway Grand Plaza provides all of the catering.
